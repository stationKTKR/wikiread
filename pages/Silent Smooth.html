Silent Smooth is a metaphysical entity which exists in the dream realm. It is generally understood to be a malevolent force, though this is impossible to verify, as there is only one reported encounter with it. It is possible that Silent Smooth is actually an ill omen and does not bring about harm in of itself, seeing as the witness was already being attacked before Silent Smooth appeared.

Origin

The only confirmed encounter with Silent Smooth was posted on Reddit:

[11:45 PM June 17, 2020] I was warned about this guy called 'Silent Smooth' while I was laying on my belly and being beaten up by nothing. Then I was told, "If you think about Silent Smooth, he WILL attack." Then I tried to move my fingers but they didn't move. I was then attacked by Silent Smooth. It was a bunch of punch, like pain on all over my back at a rapid pace (think the attack in Smash Bros Brawn where you just hold 'A', but it's hitting me in the back). And then the dream ends.

Silent Smooth may have originated as a brand of cigarettes from the 70's. It is hypothesized that Silent Smooth, at least in this iteration, had the appearance of a cat wearing sunglasses. If this is the case, it would make Silent Smooth a mascot in competition with Joe Camel and Bad Boe Jamo.

Controversy

Most skeptics will point out that discussion of Silent Smooth is, in and of itself, proof that he is fictional, as merely thinking about him is supposed to provoke an attack. On the other hand, believers in Silent Smooth (also known as 'Smooth Truthers') are extremely rare. This may be because to believe in Silent Smooth is to invite danger upon oneself. If they truly believe in Silent Smooth, then one must ask, what is to be gained by thinking about Silent Smooth? Why share knowledge they believe to be dangerous? do they wish to share their pain? Do they hope to gain Silent Smooth's favor by creating new victims? Or do they, like the unknown harbinger in the dream, earnestly wish only to warn us of Silent Smooth?
